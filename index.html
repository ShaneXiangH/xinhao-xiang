About Me

I am currently a student pursing Doctorate of Philosophy in Computer Science degree in University of California, Davis advised by Dr. Jiawei Zhang. Prior to that, I got my Bachelor of Engineering degree from Southern University of Science and Technology (SUSTech) advised by Dr. Yuhui Shi. My research involve in Computer Vision (3D Object Detection, Point Cloud Perception, Segmentation) and AI Robotics (Swarm Intelligence, Path Planning).
