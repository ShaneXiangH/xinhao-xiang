About Me

I am currently a student pursing Doctorate of Philosophy in Computer Science degree in University of California, Davis advised by Dr. Jiawei Zhang.

My research involve in Detection & Segmentation (3D Object Detection, Point Cloud Perception, Segmentation, Facial Landmark Detection, Multi-model Fusion, Open-vocabulary Detection, Efficient Vision Models), Generation & Reconstruction (AI Generated Video Evaluation, Multi-modal Language Models, 3D Reconstruction Acceleration), and AI Robotics (Swarm Intelligence, Path Planning, Multi-target Optimization).
